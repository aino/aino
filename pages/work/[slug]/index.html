Samsøe Samsøe is a Danish fashion brand founded in 1993. The brand is known for its minimalistic and Scandinavian design. Samsøe Samsøe has a strong presence in the Nordic countries and is expanding globally.

Aino has been working with Samsøe Samsøe since 2019. The collaboration started with a replatforming project where we migrated the brand from Magento to Centra. The replatforming project was followed by a continuous development partnership where we have been working on improving the brand's e-commerce platform.
