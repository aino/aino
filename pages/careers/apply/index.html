ApplyWe are overly excited that you are interested in working with us! We get quite a few applications, so to make it easier for you to cut through the noise, we have created this very simple guide. We hope it will help you to get a better understanding of what we are looking for and how to apply.

001Review the requirements

Please make sure you meet all the requirements before applying for a position. If you have any questions, feel free to contact us. You will need to know Swedish and English fluently as well as be able to relocate to Gothenburg.

002Prepare your documents

A CV as PDF and link to a portfolio is required for all applications. Please make sure to include your contact information and any relevant experience.

003Send your application

When sending your application, you may add a cover letter if you wish, but make it short and to the point. Please avoid using AI to write for you, it will lower the chances for us to read it. You may write in either Swedish or English.

  2. Attach a CV and portfolio link
  3. Write a short, human-written cover letter
  4. Send your application to work@aino.agency
