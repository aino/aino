Apply to Aino

1. Review the requirements


Please make sure you meet all the requirements before applying for a position. If you have any questions, feel free to contact us.

You will need to know Swedish and English fluently as well as be able to relocate to Gothenburg.

2. Prepare your documents


A CV as PDF and link to a portfolio is required for all applications. Please make sure to include your contact information and any relevant experience.

3. Send your application


When sending your application, you may add a cover letter if you wish, but make it short and to the point. Please avoid using AI to write for you, it will lower the chances for us to read it. You may write in either Swedish or English.

We are looking forward to hearing from you!
